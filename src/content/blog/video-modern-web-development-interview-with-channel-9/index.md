I had the privilege to sit down with [Seth Juarez](http://twitter.com/sethjuarez) from [Channel 9](https://channel9.msdn.com/Events/Seth-on-the-Road) at the [AngleBrackets](http://anglebrackets.org) conference in Las Vegas (fall 2015) and talk about modern web development and the main technologies that drive it. We talked about a lot of different topics ranging from TypeScript, Angular and Aurelia on the client-side to Node.js and ASP.NET 5 on the server-side. Seth’s a great interview host (and a super cool guy to hang out with) and I really enjoyed talking with him about modern technology in today’s world. Check out the interview below.
